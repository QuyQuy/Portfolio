About me

My name is Quy Truong and I am a web developer with a passion for technology and designing products that are meaningful and enjoyable for users. I received my degree from Arizona State University where I majored in Interdisciplinary studies. Interdisciplinary studies allowed me to do duel studies in both psychology and health sciences. I love understanding people’s thought process and figuring out what influences people’s behavior. Upon graduation, Circle 8 production offered me a sales job. Although I enjoyed my time in sales, I eventually found my day to day life becoming routine and I felt like I wasn’t being challenged.

That’s when I first started to read articles about programming I found over the internet. The more I researched about programming the more my passion for coding grew. After taking online courses for a couple months I wanted to take my technical skills to the next level. That’s when I decided to make the jump and attend LearningFuze. During my time at LearningFuze I learned best coding practices in web development using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git, jQuery, React, and Redux. Deciding to switch careers to Web Development has been one of the best decisions of my life. I am very excited to have found a career that’s never stagnant and one where you are constantly challenged. For anyone that is looking to learn more about myself and my experience. Feel free to contact me and I would be more than happy to answer any questions you may have.
